## Changelog

### Improvements

- **Connectors:** Snowflake connector now quotes identifiers in GET_DDL queries to support tables with special characters in their names [#26007](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/26007)
- **Connectors:** Snowflake connector now skips fetching definitions of Snowflake stages as they are not relevant for metadata ingestion [#26009](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/26009)
- **Ingestion:** Improved memory management in dbt ingestion to prevent out-of-memory errors when listing files from GCS [#26105](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/26105)

### Fixes

- **Connectors:** Fixed CSV parsing and table name errors during Datalake metadata ingestion [#25953](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/25953)
- **Governance:** Fixed scroll functionality in Data Product pages with large descriptions [#26040](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/26040)
- **UI:** Fixed global domain filter translation not updating when changing application language [#25982](https://github.com/open-metadata/OpenMetadata/pull/25982)
